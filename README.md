# Under The Eaves

**A gamified mood companion that translates workplace stress into empathetic, game-like language.**

---

## 📖 Project Overview

*Under The Eaves* is a conceptual product designed for professionals navigating high-pressure work environments. It transforms the often-overwhelming experience of workplace stress into a gentle, game-like interaction — using narrative, visual metaphors, and playful feedback to foster emotional awareness and resilience.

Instead of counting steps or calories, users collect moments of calm, reflect on daily emotional patterns, and receive encouraging “missions” tailored to their current mood state.

---

## 🎯 Core Features

- **Mood Tracking via Game Mechanics**: Log your stress level using intuitive icons (e.g., 🌧️ for heavy stress, ☁️ for light tension). Each entry earns you “raindrops” — virtual currency representing emotional recovery.
- **Empathetic Feedback System**: The system responds with personalized messages and mini-stories based on your mood history. For example:
  > “You’ve weathered three storms today. Let’s take shelter under this umbrella — here’s a breathing exercise.”
- **Narrative-Driven Missions**: Daily micro-challenges like “Pause for 2 minutes and notice your surroundings” or “Write one thing you’re grateful for,” designed to build small wins.
- **Visual Progress Journal**: A dashboard showing your emotional journey over time, visualized as a weather map or garden growing through seasons.

---

## 🛠️ How It Works (Prototype)

1. **Input Your Mood**: Select an emoji or slider that best represents how you feel right now.
2. **Receive Feedback**: The system generates a short narrative or suggestion based on your recent entries.
3. **Complete Missions**: Accept optional mini-tasks to earn rewards or unlock new content.
4. **Review Progress**: See your emotional trends and celebrate milestones.

> *Note: This is a prototype/demo version focused on conceptual design and user experience flow. Full implementation would integrate with calendar APIs, wearable data, or journaling tools.*

---

## 💡 Why This Matters

Modern workplaces reward productivity but rarely acknowledge emotional labor. *Under The Eaves* doesn’t aim to “fix” stress — it invites users to **observe, name, and gently redirect** their inner weather. By gamifying emotional awareness, it makes self-care feel less like a chore and more like play.
